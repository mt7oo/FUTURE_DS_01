 ## Insights

Revenue shows a clear upward trend from 2014 to 2018. Despite short-term fluctuations, the overall growth is consistent as shown by the trend line and moving average. Sales peak towards the end of each year, especially in December, indicating strong seasonal demand.

## Business Recommendation

The business should increase inventory, staffing, and marketing efforts during year-end periods to maximize revenue. Additionally, analyzing causes of mid-year dips could help stabilize performance throughout the year.

## 3. Category Performance (Sales vs Profit)

## Visualization

<img width="1920" height="1080" alt="Category performance" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ef5dda56-5514-4eb8-bf39-f0d23935f667" />


## Insights

All categories generate good sales, but their profit levels are different. Technology has both high sales and high profit, making it the strongest category. Office Supplies also performs well, while Furniture has good sales but much lower profit compared to others.

## Business Recommendation

The business should focus more on Technology and Office Supplies as they bring strong profit. For Furniture, the company should review costs or pricing because even though it sells well, it is not generating enough profit.
